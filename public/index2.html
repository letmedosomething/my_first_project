Why we need your location:

To give you accurate pickup and to prevent fraud, we require precise GPS location only. We do not accept approximate tower or IP-based location. Your location is used only to match you with a driver and is not shared with third parties.

How this works:

  • Click Allow below — your browser will ask permission.
  • If you grant permission, we will request high-accuracy GPS coordinates.
  • If GPS accuracy is good (≤ 50 m), you'll be taken to the next page.
  • If not, we'll ask you to enable GPS and retry (no coarse results accepted).
